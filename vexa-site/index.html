The companion constitution

Three articles the companion cannot amend.

Security is not a feature bolted onto the surface — it is the surface. These are the standing rules, enforced below the UI.

  1. Article I

    The database is read-only.

    Every connection sets default_transaction_read_only with tight query timeouts. There is no write path — not for the companion, not for the person running it.

  2. Article II

    Authority stays with VEX.

    Waiting-state clicks launch or focus VEX. No approval IPC exists. The consequential decision is never delegated to, or reachable from, the companion.

  3. Article III

    The surface holds no secrets.

    Localhost only — 127.0.0.1, or nothing. No vault unlock, keystore, wallet, tool arguments, fund access, or trading code lives on this side of the line.
